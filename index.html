WBJEE 2022

For the 2022–2023 academic session, after having conducted the Common Entrance Examination (WBJEE-2022) the BOARD will conduct web-based online counselling (e-counselling) for admission to Undergraduate Courses in Engineering & Technology, Pharmacy and Architecture in Universities, Govt. Colleges and Self-Financed Institutes in the State of West Bengal

Tentative date to start online registration: 26th August 2022 afternoon.
